Who is Lunay:

Lunay is a famous Reggaeton Singer. He was born on October 4, 2000 and his birthplace is Puerto Rico. Lunay is also well known as, Reggaeton artist from Puerto Rico whose popular freestyle videos on Facebook racked up millions of views. He parlayed that success into an music career and was signed by multi-platinum producers Chris Jeday and Gaby Music. 

Lunay is originated from Puerto Rico. In 2019, he released a remix of his popular hit “Soltera” featuring Daddy Yankee and Bad Bunny.
