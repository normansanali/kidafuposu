WWE Outperforms AEW In Live Attendance Numbers In Third Week Of October 2023

WWE and AEW just wrapped up another week of television shows, and there are a lot of numbers to go through. Not only are television ratings fun to argue about, but live attendance numbers are also a very strong indicator of a company’s success.

Wrestle Tix reports that this week, WWE SmackDown on 10/20 garnered the highest attendance with 14,149 tickets sold. WWE Raw at the Paycom Center in Oklahoma City, OK had 7,902 tickets sold on 10/16.

AEW Dynamite on 10/18 saw 4,457 tickets sold at the Fort Bend Epicenter in Rosenberg, Texas. On the other hand, AEW Collision at the FedEx Forum in Memphis, TN was the least attended show with 2,933 tickets sold.

As for house shows, WWE Sunday Stunner in Laredo, TX has 1,068 available tickets, with around 2,683 distributed. WWE Saturday Night’s Main Event in Edinburg, TX distributed 5,629 tickets.

We will have to see how WWE and AEW’s attendance numbers turn out in the future. It seems that WWE’s booming period is going to continue, but AEW is still doing what they can to attract attention.
