Dr. Uzma Syeda, MD Grapevine, TX

Dr. Uzma Syeda completed her post doctoral fellowship in Rheumatology at Case Western Reserve University at Metrohealth in Cleveland Ohio. She was awarded as best graduate based on her academic performance during medical school. She completed her residency at Michael Reese Hospital affiliate of University of Illinois at Chicago. She has presented in national conference and published in peer reviewed journal. Dr. Syeda is board certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine (ABIM) and by the ABIM Subspecialty Board on Rheumatology.

Her other clinical interests include the diagnosis and treatment of autoimmune disease including Lupus, inflammatory arthritis and Ankylosing Spondylitis. Outside of medicine, she enjoys spending time with her family, reading and travelling.
