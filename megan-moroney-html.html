Megan Moroney - Age, Family, Bio

Country singer and songwriter who has risen to fame for her songs "Tennessee Orange", "Fix You Too" and "Hair Salon." "Tennessee Orange" has accumulated more than 50 million streams on Spotify. In 2022, she released her first EP, titled Pistol Made of Roses. 

Before Fame

During her freshman year at the University of Georgia, an opportunity to open for an established country artist at the iconic Georgia Theatre inspired her to write her very first original song. 

Trivia

She posts content about her music career and lifestyle on her TikTok account megmoroney, which has gained over 850,000 followers. She is known to share clips from her concerts and live performances, as well, on TikTok. The content on her page has racked up more than 17 million total likes.

Family Life

She is the sister of Connor Moroney. She has performed with her father and her brother, Brian Moroney. 

Associated With

In October 2022, she performed alongside Dan Smyers and Shay Mooney.
