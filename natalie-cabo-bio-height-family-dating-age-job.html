Austin's Natalie Cabo Bio, Height, Family, Dating. Age

Meet Natalie Cabo who stars alongside seven others on TwentySomethings: Austin, a recent release on Netflix.

The coming-of-age reality series set in Austin, Texas, premiered on 10 December 2021, follows a diverse group of young people who relocate to Austin to live together in the same house all the while navigating their life away from home.

Now, the rest of the writing is a ‘Natalie Cabo Bio’ that covers her height, age, family, job, and dating-related stories.

Natalie Cabo On Twentysomethings: Austin

Naturally, Natalie was very excitedly waiting for TwentySomethings: Austin to release on 10 December 2021 and for everyone to one watch her build tension and find love as well as navigate her life in a way she had never before done it.

For one, she wanted to live away from her parents and experience for once not having parents survey her like all the time.

Natalie can be seen on the trailer spilling how she never had a chance to just move out and be on her own. Also, she wanted Austin to break that pattern because — “They say Austin is weird, and I love that because I’m weird too.”

Is Natalie Cabo Dating Anyone?

It’s been reported that Natalie never had a boyfriend before. That, growing up in a strict Latin household, she never had that chance as her every move was watched by her parents.

From the look of her social media, she is shy about taking pictures of herself but still she loves to party with her friends. And (of course) she kept hinting that she is as much single as one can be.

Natalie Cabo Family

Natalie Cabo’s parents have a lighting business in Miami that they started from nothing. And she said that was the reason she actually majored in Business. “I felt the responsibility to continue their legacy. I got an associate’s degree in business and when I transferred to UNC, I started studying management and society to get the managerial aspect.” That, on the other hand, she minored in social and economic justice because she is very passionate about politics and advocacy.

To Natalie, life shines because she has her mom, Doris Cabo, by her side.

Every now and then, Natalie also mentions her dad, Andres Cabo, on her social accounts.

Natalie grew up alongside a bunch of sisters of similar age. She has three siblings named Angie Maria, Rina Esteli, and Doris Isabel.

Natalie Cabo Height

Natalie stands below 5’4” tall in height.

How Old Is Natalie Cabo?

When Natalie Cabo first showed up in TwentySomethings in 2021, she was 26 years old.

Natalie Cabo Job

Natalie Cabo is a theatre actor and producer, currently performing under LAB! Theatre. He bio introduces her also as an Arts in Public Service Fellow, Buckley Public Service scholar, and Florida Thespian Festival competitor for four years with superior ratings in competition pieces.

She started performing when she was really young and got her first main role in 11th grade as Anne Sullivan, Helen Keller’s teacher in The Miracle Worker.

While she also has knack for business, Natalie says she still cannot stop acting. “When I thought that maybe I would only do business, something felt incomplete. I hope I can combine the two somehow — theater administration or something.”

Anyway, for now, Natalie believes that her ultimate goal is to own a nonprofit theater company that will produce shows to illicit political dialogue and create awareness for social issues.
