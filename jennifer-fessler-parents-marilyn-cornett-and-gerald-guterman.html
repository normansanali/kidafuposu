Meet Gerald Guterman, Jennifer Fessler Father

On June 19, 2022, Jennifer posted a decades-old black and white photo of her dad (young at the time) carrying his newly born daughter in his hand to her Instagram. In the caption, she wrote, “Happy Father’s Day Gerald Guterman! Not great lighting but I love this pic and I love you.”

  • Gerald Guterman Age

As per radaris.com, Gerald Guterman was born in June 1942. That way, his age was 80 at the time of this writing.

  • Gerald Guterman Job

Jennifer Fessler’s mother, Gerald Guterman, is a real estate developer and investor by profession. Gerald is one of the largest multi-family apartment owners/operators (78,000+- apartments) and condominium converters in the United States. Currently, he serves Guterman Partners, LLC, as its chairman and CEO.

Gerald began his real estate career at the age of 18 as a night porter for Fred Trump, the father of Donald Trump. Beginning in the 1980s, he became one of the largest owners and property managers in the United States, owning and operating over 18,600 rental apartments in 55 residential communities for his own long-term interests, together with an additional 16,000 apartments which he purchased and operated for condominium and cooperative conversion in 21 residential communities in sixteen states.

Brooklyn native Gerald also planned, organized, and negotiated the acquisition of the largest single, direct purchase of office properties in the history of The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation as well as thirty-one multiple tenant office buildings containing more than 3,250,000 net rentable square feet of office space located in Eight States.

Through 2016, Gerald has acquired 14 luxury hotels, located in New York, New Orleans, London, Europe, and the Middle East, as well the purchasing the only magnetic monorail system in the United States. Till today, Gerald continues to develop condominium housing.

In September 1986, Gerald had become quite the highlight of the news after he chartered the famed ocean liner Queen Elizabeth 2 (QE2) along with a crew of over 1,000, for his 13-year-old son Jason’s bar mitzvah party.
