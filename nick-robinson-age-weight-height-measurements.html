Nick Robinson Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Nick Robinson was born on March 22, 1995 in Seattle, Washington, U.S.. He started acting during his early teens, performing on stage. He eventually ventured to acting on television and film. In 2010, he made his television debut as part of the main cast of Melissa & Joey. He starred Ryder Scanlon on the said show, playing the part from 2010 to 2015.

While on the show, he made his cinematic debut in the movie The Kings of Summer. He then played a supporting role of Zach Mitchellin the 2015 movie Jurassic World. The following year, he starred as Ben Parish in The 5th Wave.
Subsequently, he starred in the films Being Charlie, Everything, Everything and Krystal.
