GoFundMe; Eric Bonilla Jr. died in I-90 car accident

A valuable life was lost on Thursday in a tragic four-vehicle collision on the westbound lanes of Interstate 90 at Meacham Road close to Schaumburg.

A 28-year-old citizen of Schaumburg was killed in the collision between a truck tractor semi-trailer and a passenger car, according to Illinois State Police.

Eric Bonilla was the deceased, according to the Cook County Medical Examiner’s Office.

Around 11 a.m., the accident happened, leaving those who knew Eric with a huge hole in their hearts. Eric was a vivacious and enthusiastic person with a full life ahead of him, full of ambitions, aspirations, and the desire to change the world. His devoted parents, Eric Sr. and Felicitas Bonilla, his brave sister Irene Bonilla, and his closest friends will all mourn him dearly.

GoFundme have been created to help generate funds for the family

Hello, On behalf of the Bonilla family we are seeking funds to help with any Memorial/Funeral Services that the family will need through this difficult time. Eric’s life was tragically ended on 6/29/2023 around 11 in the morning; our dear friend was 28 years young with a whole life to live and give. Eric is survived by his wonderful parents Eric Sr. and Felicitas Bonilla; courageous sister Irene Bonilla; and his closest friends. Eric has touched so many lives through achievements, adventure and charisma. To make this process as smooth as possible we ask that any donation big or small will go a long way for the best service. This is for OUR Eric Bonilla whom may rest in peace.
