Inside Madonna's Younger Ex-Boyfriend Jesus Luz's Lavish Lifestyle

Highlights

  • Madonna's relationship with Jesus Luz launched his career.
  • Luz has an outrageous and lavish lifestyle.
  • Luz spends a significant amount of money on tattoos.

Madonna had a reputation for her preference for dating younger men, and her status as a music icon subjected her to intense media scrutiny regarding her personal life. Madonna engaged in numerous high-profile relationships and marriages, many of whom were notably younger than her.

One of Madonna's ex-boyfriends, Jesus Luz, was extremely influenced and inspired by his relationship with her. Their romantic relationship started in a magazine shot in 2008, and after they split in 2010, Luz catapulted to stardom all because of his involvement with the Queen of Pop.

Madonna's Ex Jesus Luz Live An Affluent Lifestyle After She Launched His Career

Madonna reportedly ended her relationship with Jesus Luz due to their significant age gap and lack of common interests. According to a source who spoke to The Sydney Morning Herald, Madonna and Luz's separation was amicable when they agreed that it was time to conclude their 13-month relationship.

In his interview with the Brazilian Sunday TV show Fantastico, Luz stated, "She is a person I admire a lot, a friend in my life, who I worked with, and who I stay in contact with. Beyond that, I can't say anymore. We're friends." According to AFP, Luz has since gained recognition after dating Madonna, with reports stating that Luz has already purchased an apartment in Rio.

Luz's relationship with Madonna revolutionized his life and propelled his modeling, acting, and DJing careers forward. While labeled as Madonna's "ToyBoy," Luz remained unfazed, and will forever cherish the Queen of Pop's role in granting him a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.

Luz proudly declared to Revista Quem Magazine the relationship he had with Madonna:

"I consider this a blessing; she was my girlfriend and my manager at the same time… She inspired me to be the human being that I am today, more open to the unknown and more understanding of differences… In our relationship, l met her as a human being, not as an icon."

Jesus Luz Spent Over $3,600 On His Tattoos

Jesus Luz admitted that he began getting tattooed at the age of 17, and revealed that he doesn't know the actual number of tattoos he got, he only estimated that around 15% of his body was covered in ink.

In July 2023, Luz decided to add more tattoos on his body, which are two large designs with an alien theme on his left forearm and another inspired by angels on his left thigh. Luz sought the assistance of specialized medical professionals since the tattoos required anesthesia to manage the pain during the lengthy procedure.
