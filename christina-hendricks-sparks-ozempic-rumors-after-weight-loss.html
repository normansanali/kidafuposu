Fans speculate Christina Hendricks is on Ozempic as she shows off slimmed-down figure

Christina Hendricks sparked speculation that she is on the popular weight-loss drug Ozempic after flaunting her slimmed-down figure.

The “Mad Men” actress, 48, shared a slideshow of photos on Instagram Monday in which she showed off different angles of her smaller frame as she rocked a stunning black lace midi-dress with puffed sleeves.

“Dinner with friends,” she captioned the post, which displayed her chest in the low-cut ensemble.

But several trolls flooded the comments section to share unfounded theories that Hendricks, 48, turned to the medication taking Hollywood by storm to accomplish her svelte physique.

“literally everyone is on ozempic ☠️,” one person commented on the post.

“she’s on ozempic,” a second person surmised, with another chiming in, “Lay off the ozempic @actuallychristinahendricks.”

“Dang that’s some weight loss,” a fourth jabbed.

“You’re losing too much weight 😢,” another claimed.

“You are loosing wayyyyyyyyyy too much weight. Some women can do stick skinny & some look drawn-out. You are way too beautiful to be a stick, you are a true women,” one more unsolicited commenter added.

But many of the “Good Girls” star’s fans showered her with compliments and words of encouragement.

“You look maahvolous!” one supporter commented.

“THIS IS UNACCEPTABLE HOTNESS,” a second wrote.

“You are looking beautiful, Christina,” someone else agreed, with another adding, “Such a beautiful and elegant lady!”

“Getting fit for your wedding dress?” another asked.

Hendricks is gearing up for her wedding to her fiancé, George Bianchini, after announcing in March that they got engaged.

“We proposed to each other and we said yes!!!,” she captioned a sweet photo of herself and Bianchini at the time. “I will love and care for him forever.”

The lovebirds met while working together on the set of NBC’s “Good Girls,” which ran from 2018 to 2021. Hendricks starred as Beth Boland in the comedy-drama series, while Bianchini, 54, worked as a camera operator for the show.

The pair sparked relationship rumors when they attended Christina Siriano’s “People Are People” exhibition together in 2021, but it wasn’t until March of the next year that Hendricks posted her first photo with Bianchini on social media.

.

Hendricks and Bianchini started dating shortly after her 10-year marriage to Geoffrey Arend came to an end in 2019.

She filed for divorce from the “Madam Secretary” star, 45, that December.

“Today, we take our next step together, but on separate paths,” the former couple said in a joint statement at the time. “We will be taking time to rediscover ourselves in this transition. We thank you for your patience and support in giving us the space to do so.”

The exes met in 2007 after being introduced by Hendricks’ “Mad Men” co-star Vincent Kartheiser.

A rep for Hendricks did not immediately respond to Page Six’s request for comment on the Ozempic speclation.
