T-Pain and Lil Wayne Have Finally Answered Our Prayers and Released T-Wayne

Lil Wayne and T-Pain.

It finally happened. After doodling hearts around their names in our notebooks, T-Pain and Lil Wayne have released the joint album T-Wayne, their oft-teased mixtape of unreleased tracks. The idea for the project originated nearly a decade ago, and T-Pain and Wayne have both talked around it ever since. And just when it seemed like it would never happen, T-Wayne has arrived with eight new collabs, including “He Rap He Sang,” which might be the most stellar song title of 2017.
