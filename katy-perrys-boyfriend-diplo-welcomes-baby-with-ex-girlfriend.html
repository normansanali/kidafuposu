Katy Perrys boyfriend, Diplo, welcomes baby with ex-girlfriend

If everything continues to go well in Katy Perry and Diplo’s relationship, she could officially be the stepmother of two: His ex-girlfriend, Kathryn Lockhart, welcomed son Lazer Lee Louis Pentz last week. Diplo and Kathryn are also parents to 4-year-old Lockett.

The DJ hinted at the new arrival on Saturday when he said he was going to meet a new member of the family…

Diplo reportedly broke up with Kathryn when she was two months pregnant, shortly before he was publicly linked to Katy. Remarkably, the former pair seems to be on pretty good terms as he was “spending time with his ex and his little boy, buying baby supplies,” according to Us Weekly.

E! News reported last month that Katy feels “in control” of her relationship with Diplo — even though the circumstances are a bit complicated.

“He knows she’s in charge and respects her,” an insider said. “She keeps tabs on him… But she trusts him and that’s the most important thing to her.”
