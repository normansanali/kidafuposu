Comethazine Declares His Love For &quot;DEMI&quot; Lovato

Comethazine is not playing around. The rapper born in the golden year of 1998 is cognizant of where he places demographically. His latest banger "DEMI" is the story of young bachelor fending off the county's gentleman callers one after the other, the object of his affection being Demi Lovato, the ex-Disney star who drastically came of age in the last few years.

In "DEMI" he promises to get her name tatted on his shoulder should they link up. Comethazine seems unopposed to making music with her too, but it would come at a cost of acquisition. Even though the young rapper makes a ton of lewd remarks one after the other, he is the first to admit that he might a few years away from realizing his dream of netting his dream catch: "I just wanna fuck Demi Lovato when I'm older."

"DEMI" is produced by BHUNNA, whom he has worked with in the past on records such as "Blessings," released a few months back. Enjoy.
