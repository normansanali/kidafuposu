Big X Tha Plug - Age, Family, Bio

Dallas-based rapper known for his songs like "Safehouse" and "Mr. Trouble." His music has gained him over 2 million listeners on Spotify. He is known to share some of his personal life and clips of his music and performances on his bigxthaplug Instagram page.

Before Fame

He first joined Instagram in June 2013 and was later verified in March 2022.

Trivia

His song "Safehouse" is featured on his 2023 album AMAR. It has received more than 13 million plays on Spotify. He has amassed 340,000 subscribers on YouTube, where he posts his songs, music videos, and TikTok compilations of others using his music on the platform.

Family Life

He was born in Texas. He has mentioned looking after his son in a couple of his songs.

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow rapper Maxo Kream.
