Ellen Burstyn Family – Spouse and Children

Ellen’s married life is as exotic as her personality. Ellen Burstyn married thrice with Neil Nephew (m. 1964–1972), Paul Roberts (m. 1958–1961), and William Alexander (m. 1950–1957).

Burstyn divorced Neil as he was schizophrenic and suffered from bouts of anger. Later in 1978, he killed himself by jumping from the window of his ninth-floor Manhattan apartment.

Previously, she married Bill Alexander. The couple tied the knot in 1950 and divorced in 1957 after the relationship took a rocky turn.

She also had a brief one-year marriage relationship with Paul Roberts and together have an adopted child named Jefferson.
