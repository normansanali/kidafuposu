Kim Zolciak-Biermann is an American television personality, singer, producer, and actress who appeared as one of the original housewives on the reality television series The Real Housewives of Atlanta. She also appeared as a contestant on the 21st season of the dancing competition series Dancing with the Stars in 2015 and has released a handful of tracks which include the likes of Tardy for the Party, Tardy for the Party – The Remix, Google Me, Wig (Wish I Gave a S**t), and Love Me First.
