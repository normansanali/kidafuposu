Biography

Jahir Ocampo was born in Mexico on January 12, 1990. Mexican diver who is known for having competed in both the World Aquatic Championships and the Pan American Games as a representative for Mexico. He is best recognized for having won the Gold Medal at the 2015 Pan American Games in Toronto in the 3 m Springboard synch event. He is known for having been a frequent partner of fellow Mexican diver Rommel Pacheco. He first began diving and swimming at an early age but earned international attention in 2013 when he competed in his first World Championship event. That year, he took home the bronze medal in Barcelona in the 3 m springboard sync event.
