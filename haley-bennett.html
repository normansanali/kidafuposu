Haley Bennett Career, Professional Life

Haley Bennett made her stage debut in 2007 in the role of Cora Corman in the musical ‘Music and Lyrics.’ Hugh Grant, Drew Barrymore, Brad Garrett, Kristen Johnston, and Campbell Scott starred in this film. Following the success of this role, she went on to play Kendall in ‘College,’ Molly Hartley in ‘The Haunting of Molly Hartley,’ and Lisa in ‘Marley and Me’ in 2008. Similarly, in 2009, she played Julie Campbell in ‘The Hole,’ Stella in ‘Kaboom,’ and Charlotte in ‘Arcadia Lost.’ In 2010, she played Charlotte in ‘Arcadia Lost.’ She also played Natasha in ‘Deep Powder,’ Ruby in ‘After the Fall,’ Justine Wills in ‘Kristy,’ Eva in ‘Lost in the White City,’ and Mandy in ‘The Equalizer’ in 2014. The following year, she starred as Estelle in the film ‘Hardcore Henry.’

Furthermore, in 2016, she appeared as Ellie Briess, Emma Cullen, Megan Hipwell, and Mamie Murphy in films such as ‘A Kind of Murder,’ ‘The Magnificent Seven,’ ‘And the Girl on the Train,’ and ‘Rules Don’t Apply.’ She has also played Saskia Schumann in ‘Than You for Your Service’ in 2017, Hunter in ‘Swallow,’ Rachel Reiter in ‘The Red Sea Diving Resort’ in 2019, Charlotte Russell in ‘The Devil All the Time’ and Lindsay in ‘Hillbilly Elegy’ in 2020. In addition, she will be seen as Roxanne in the upcoming American musical film ‘Cyrano.’
