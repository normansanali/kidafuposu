Celebrity birthdays April 28: Jessica Alba, Penelope Cruz and Lady Helen Taylor

A very happy birthday to Hollywood stars Jessica Alba and Penelope Cruz, and the Duke of Kent's daughter, Lady Helen Taylor. The trio are all celebrating their special day on Saturday 28 April and to make the occasion, we've taken a look at what they're famous for, and what they've been up to recently…

Jessica Alba turns 37

The Fantastic Four actress is turning 37 on Saturday and will no doubt be showered with love from her husband Cash Warren and the couple's adorable children: daughters Honor Marie and Haven Garner and baby boy Hayes. Jessica recently gave birth to her son in December but that hasn't stopped her from slowing down. The Honest Company founder has been hosting talks with nanny to the stars, Connie Simpson, and has also been promoting Connie's new book.

Penelope Cruz turns 44

The Spanish beauty shot to fame after starring in Jamón Jamón as a teenager. Penelope, or Pe as she is known by her friends, quickly became a muse for director Pedro Almodovar and went on to star in several of his films, including Volver, Vicky Cristina Barcelona and All About My Mother. Penelope, who is married to Javier Bardem, most recently starred in Loving Pablo and Murder on the Orient Express. Next month, fans will see Penelope and her husband Javier in Everybody Knows, which will open the 2018 Cannes Film Festival.

Lady Helen Taylor turns 54

Lady Helen, the daughter of Prince Edward and his wife the Duchess of Kent, is currently 41st in line to the British throne. Her father is the grandson of King George V and Queen Mary and is first cousins with the Queen. Lady Helen and her husband Timothy Verner Taylor, who married at St George's Chapel in Windsor, have four children together – two sons Columbus and Cassius and two daughters Eloise and Estella.
