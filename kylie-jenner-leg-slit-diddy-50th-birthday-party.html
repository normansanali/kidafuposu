Kylie Jenner Wore a Stunning Black Sequin Gown with a Leg Slit

The Kardashian-Jenners are professionals when it comes to wearing revealing looks, and last night was no exception.

Kim Kardashian stepped out with siblings Kylie and Khloé for Sean Combs' 50th birthday party, which took place in Los Angeles and was presented by Ciroc Vodka. While Kim's champagne gold ballgown was simply stunning, Kylie Jenner captured the world's attention with a fitted dress covered in black sequins. The cutaway bustier made Kylie's look all the more revealing, as did her thigh-high leg slit.

Kylie posed with her sister, Khloé Kardashian, and both reality stars looked completely gorgeous. Khloé opted for a sparkling gold suit by Bryan Hearns.

Kim wowed fellow partygoers by wearing a vintage Vivienne Westwood champagne gold gown, which apparently came from the designer's archives. The three Kardashian-Jenner sisters in attendance at the bash posed with birthday boy Diddy, also known as Sean Combs. Everyone's sparkly outfits suggested that they're already in a festive mood, and ready for the holiday season.

Kylie's appearance at Diddy's party follows her surprise performance of viral hit "Rise and Shine," alongside Justin Bieber.
