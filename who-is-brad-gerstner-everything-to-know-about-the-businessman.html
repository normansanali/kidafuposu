Who Is Brad Gerstner? Everything To Know About The Businessman

Investor Brad Gerstner’s bio is yet to be published on Wikipedia. However, you can take some insights into his life from this article.

Brad Gerstner is a reputed businessman and an investor. He is best known as the hedge fund manager with heavy investments in many techs and travel companies.

Gerstner is the CEO and founder of the investment firm, Altimeter. Besides, he owns part of some companies including Zoom Video, Snowflake, and Peloton.

Brad is also a law graduate, TV personality, and financial expert. He often appears in interviews and talks about his investments and current trends.

Altimeter: Who Is Brad Gerstner? Wikipedia Explored

Altimeter’s CEO, Brad Gerstner does not own a Wikipedia page.

As per LinkedIn, he studied politics, philosophy, and economics at the University of Oxford. Then, he earned a BA in Economics and Political Science from Wabash College.

Brad also gained a Juris Doctor (JD) from Indiana University School of Law. Moreover, in 2000, he completed his MBA at Harvard Business School.

Gerstner began his career as an entrepreneur. He founded Wawasee Boat Care and ran it for 3 years. Unfortunately, his idea did not work but he learned many things.

In 1996, Brad started working as an attorney for the Ice Miller. Two years later, he became the Deputy Secretary of State for the State of Indiana.

Gerstner also became the Co-CEO of the National Leisure Group. After serving there for 3 years, he joined Openlist as a co-founder as well as a CEO.

Next, Brad was employed by PAR Capital, Zillow, and Orbitz Worldwide respectively. Finally, in 2008, he established the investing company, Altimeter Capital.
