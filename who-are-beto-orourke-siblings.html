Who Are Beto ORourke Siblings?

American politician, Robert Francis “Beto” O’Rourke was born on September 26th, 1972. H e was born at Hotel Deiu Hospital in El Paso, Texas in the United States of America.

From 2013 to 2019, he represented Texas’s 16th congressional district in the United States. Democratic Party member Beto O’Rourke ran for the U.S. Senate in 2018, for the Democratic nomination for president in 2020, and is the party’s choice for governor of Texas in 2022.

O’Rourke is a Columbia University and Woodberry Forest School graduate who was raised in an El Paso, Texas, political family. He started a brief musical career as the bass guitarist in the post-hardcore band Foss while attending Columbia.

He returned to El Paso and started a business after finishing college. He was elected to the El Paso City Council in 2005 and held that position until 2011. During his first year in office, he served as mayor pro tempore.

Who are Beto O’Rourke siblings?

Charlotte O’Rourke and Erin O’Rourke are the known siblings of Beto O’Rourke.
