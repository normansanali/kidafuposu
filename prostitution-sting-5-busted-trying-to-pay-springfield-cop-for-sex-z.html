Prostitution Sting: 5 Busted Trying To Pay Springfield Cop For Sex

Springfield police posed as online escorts, where men would offer to pay money in exchange for sexual services inside a hotel room. But unbeknownst to them, the room was filled with Springfield police officers and Hampden County Sheriff's deputies.

Officers arrested five men from 1 until 7 p.m. as they showed up to the designated hotel room at the prearranged time. 

All were charged with purchasing prostitution. 

They are: 

  • Jason Assad, age 41, of Springfield
  • Jeffrey Premo, age 49, of Palmer
  • Frank Fay, age 29, of Westfield
  • Myrick White, age 43, of Springfield
  • Edwin Rivera, 34, of Chicopee

White was also charged with possession of a Class B drug, authorities said. 

Springfield police said the sting operation is to curtail the problem of prostitution in the city.
