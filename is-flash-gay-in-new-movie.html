Is Flash Gay in The New Movie? Exploring The Rumors!

The Holiday Exchange, the forthcoming movie starring Flash (Rick Cosnett), is the subject of several rumors that speculate on its LGBTQ+ inclusion.

Is Rick Cosnett going to play a gay character in the new movie, or are all the rumors false? Uncover all there is to know about Flash’s new movie in this article!

Is Flash Gay in New Movie?

The Flash Star Rick Cosnett is Indeed Playing a Gay Character in the New Movie “The Holiday Exchange.” In the forthcoming LGBTQ+ holiday romance The Holiday Exchange, which also stars Taylor Frey from It Chapter Two, Cosnett has scored a key role.

The movie centers on a single homosexual businessman, Wilde (Frey), who has just sold his firm and must now face Christmas alone. Wilde and a man called Oliver (played by Cosnett) wind themselves exchanging homes in a twist reminiscent of The Holiday.

The two will meet local men when they arrive at their separate homes, who will add some warmth and romance to their lives. The movie will be set in both sunny LA and the icy village of Brilfax, taking still more inspiration from The Holiday.

Jake Helgren, who directed A Christmas to Treasure, will direct the film, which Frey also wrote.

The role of CCPD detective Eddie Thawne in The Flash from 2014 to 2017 and again from 2022 to 2023 made Cosnett the show’s most well-known actor. Thawne passed away in the season one finale but made appearances in seasons 2, 3, and 8.

For the current season’s finale of the program, Cosnett will reprise the iconic role. The show’s executive producer, Eric Wallace, has warned viewers that the finale will have “lots of tears” before it airs on April 26.

Rick Cosnett in The Flash

Detective Eddie Thawne was the character that Rick Cosnett portrayed in the television show “The Flash.” Eddie Thawne appeared often in the first season and had a major role in the second.

He was first presented as a potential love interest for Iris West’s character and joined Grant Gustin’s Barry Allen, better known as the eponymous character, the Flash, as a detective at the Central City Police Department.

Eddie Thawne participated in many of the plotlines during the series, including learning the identity of the evil Reverse-Flash, falling in love with Iris, and ultimately giving his life to protect the city from a deadly singularity.
