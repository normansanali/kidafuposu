Kim Kardashian's First Pregnancy Style: Her 5 Best Looks in Photos

Here we go again!

Kim Kardashian, 34, just announced that she’s pregnant and expecting her second child with husband Kanye West, which means that she’ll soon be rocking a bona fide baby bump in her seemingly endless supply of couture threads.

While Kardashian’s first round of maternity styles were a bit, er, controversial (those skyscraper platforms alone!), the media mogul did have some shining moments while pregnant with her first child, North West, 23 months—gorgeous styles that we think deserve a second go.

Scroll down to see Us Weekly‘s top five picks!

Glamour Girl

The Armenian beauty stunned in this Donna Karan dress while on the red carpet at Elton John’s annual Oscars bash on Feb. 24, 2013. And that oxblood lip? TDF.

Moto Marvelous

Classic Kim! Mrs. Kanye West stepped out onto the streets of NYC on Mar. 26, 2013, wearing one of her signature styles: a body-conscious, camel-colored dress, teamed with strappy heels and a fierce motorcycle jacket.

Loose and Lovely

There were comfy looks, too! While Kardashian often chose to style her bump in super-tight ensembles, she did sport this gloriously cozy, off-the-shoulder peasant dress while grabbing froyo in L.A. on April 3, 2013. 

Peach Perfection

So soft! The Dash co-founder embraced her exotic side in L.A. on April 7, 2013, when she donned a flowing, coral jumpsuit with harem pants and all-gold accessories.

Lady in Red Lace

Making an appearance at Kardashian Khaos in Las Vegas on April 13, 2013, Kardashian looked so romantic in this sleeveless red lace dress (it hugged, but it didn’t squeeze!). To complete the look, she added a perfectly matched cherry red statement lip.
