Meet Mirjam Bjorklund, Denis Shapovalov's girlfriend?

Denis Shapovalov is a fast-rising Canadian star who is gaining popularity with his incredible style of play. He is not always in the news for tennis reasons

Fans have been willing to know who the special woman in his life is.

Well, Shapovalov’s girlfriend is Mirjam Bjorklund.

They began dating in June 2019. Their relationship, however, became public on October 20, 2019.

On the same day, Shapovalov won the Stockholm Open, his lone championship to date. Following the conclusion of the final, the two exchanged a loving embrace.

They’ve been increasingly prominent on each other’s social media since their relationship became public. They frequently share photos of themselves on social media.

Meet Mirjam Bjorklund, Denis Shapovalov’s girlfriend?

Mirjam Bjorklund is a prominent tennis player from Sweden.

Mirjam Bjorklund is rated 213 in the WTA singles rankings and 285 in the WTA doubles rankings. Because she is now below the top 100, she is competing in ITF competitions rather than WTA ones.

She struggled to qualify for ITF tournaments at the start of the year, with inconsistent results. However, as the year progressed, things began to improve.

In July, Bjorklund reached the semi-finals of an ITF/Tennis Europe event in Germany and then won a similar event in Belgium. She then advanced to the finals of an ITF event in Italy but was defeated.

She finished the year on a high note by winning an ITF/USTA event, giving her two ITF singles titles. Bjorklund was also successful in doubles ranks, where she won a further two titles.

She continues to balance her tennis career with her romance with Shapovalov and hence the best is yet to come.
