Stephanie Hyam Net Worth

Stephanie Hyam

$100 thousand

Stephanie Hyam is a British actress. She debuted in the 2014 TV film Murdered by My Boyfriend. She next starred as Lily Clarke in the fantasy drama Jekyll & Hyde. After this, she played Charlotte Murray in 3 episodes of Peaky Blinders. At the same time, she had her first movie role in 2016’s Their Finest. She went on to play Heather in 2 episodes of Doctor Who and Chanel in 3 episodes of Bodyguard. This year, she played Becky Godden-Edwards in the TV series A Confession. Hyam also appeared in the 2018 feature film Stan and Ollie. Her most recent work on television was in an episode of Gentleman Jack.
