Biography:

Leigh-Allyn Baker was born on April 3, 1972 in Murray, Kentucky, USA. Her first film appearance was as Mitzi in Shrunken Heads. She has since starred in such films as Leprechaun 3, Breast Men, and The Crux.

In 1996, she made her television debut with her role as Gina in an episode of the series Almost Perfect. She then became known for her role as Ellen in the sitcom Will & Grace.

She continued to gain wider recognition among television viewers for some of her characters on the small screen, including the role of Amy Duncan in the Disney Channel Original Series Good Luck Charlie.
