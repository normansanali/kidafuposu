Teresa Ganzel Net Worth

Teresa Ganzel is an American voice actress born on March 23, 1957 in Ohio. She is best known for her work in Disney Pixar films, including Wall-E, Monsters, Inc, Up, Toy Story 3 and Inside Out. She has also done voice work for television shows, video games, and commercials.

About

Voice Actress whose credits include the Disney Pixar films Wall-E, Monsters, Inc, Up, Toy Story 3 and Inside Out.

Before Fame

She made her big screen debut in the 1981 film C.O.D. and she first gained recognition for her role in the "Tea Time Movie" skits on The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson in the early '80s.

Trivia

She plays a supporting role as the character Diana in the 1983 comedy film National Lampoon's Movie Madness.

Family Life

She married Richard Davis in 2004.

Associated With

She and John Ratzenberger are both frequently used as voice over actors for Disney Pixar features.
