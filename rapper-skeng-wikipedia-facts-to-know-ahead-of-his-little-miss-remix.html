Rapper Skeng Wikipedia

Skeng is an American rapper born to Jamaican parents in 2001 and raised in St. Catherine, Jamaica.

The 21 years old rapper has always been interested in music and has wanted to be a musician. He was inspired by a musical rivalry between Gaza’s Vybz Karteil and Gully’s Mavado.

After attending several schools during his youth, including Sen Simon Basic School, Wolmer’s High School for Boys, and St. George’s College, the DJ began to seriously focus on his musical career at his last stop, The Quality Academy.

He first recorded his music in a New Kingston studio and has recorded more than 30 tracks, including Brrp and Street Cred. Skeng is best known for his 2021 single, Gvnman Shift.

Recently, on 26 September 2022, Skeng, Trinidad, and Tobago rapper Nicki Minaj released a music video for their collaboration album, Likkle Miss Remix.
