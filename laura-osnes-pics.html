Laura Osnes Exclusive Portraits Of The Actress Hollywood Life

Laura Osnes is an American stage and TV actress. The 33-year-old rose to fame as she competed on the reality TV show, Grease: You’re the One that I Want! as part of the nation-wide search for the roles of Sandy and Danny in the Broadway revival of Grease. After winning the competition and being donned ‘Smalltown Sandy,’ Laura played Sandy on Broadway for a year, through July 2008. Post-Grease, Laura performed in starred in the Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts’ Broadway: Three Generations, a three-act evening featuring condensed versions of Girl Crazy, Bye Bye Birdie, and Side Show. Laura went on to star in South Pacific, Bonnie & Clyde and Anything Goes, before taking on the lead role as Cinderella in a modernized version of the Rodgers and Hammerstein musical, Cinderella, in 2012. Laura was nominated for a Tony Award for the role and performed through 2014. Laura’s most recent Broadway appearance was in the original show Banstand, that opened in April 2017 and closed five months later.
