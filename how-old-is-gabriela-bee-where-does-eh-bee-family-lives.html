How old is Gabriela Bee? Where does Eh Bee Family lives?

Gabriela Bee Wiki Bio

Gabriela ‘Miss Bee’ Burgos, also known as Miss Monkey, was born in Ontario, Canada on 9 September 2006 – her zodiac sign is Virgo and she holds Canadian nationality. She is a social media star, singer and a YouTuber, who is known best for being a member of the family Eh Bee, which was very active on the now-defunct network Vine and is today active on YouTube and various social media networks. The family is living in Ontario.

Early life, family and education

Gabriela is being raised alongside her brother Roberto Burgos, or ‘Mr. Monkey’, by their father Andres Burgos or ‘Papa Bee’ and their mother Rossana Burgos or ‘Mama Bee’ – the family became active on the internet in January 2013, and Gabriela’s parents are also models and businessmen, as they’ve signed contracts with various companies and brands including Johnson & Johnson, Toyota, and Pepsi.

Gabriela became interested in sports while attending elementary school, and thanks to her parents’ activities on the internet, she became interested in singing and dancing.

After noticing that she’s talented, her mother enrolled her at a singing school, while Gabriela has also been practicing her acting skills with her school’s drama club.

She has just recently in 2020 enrolled at a high school, and although she has four more years to go before matriculation, Gabriela has already expressed her desire to attend college.

Career as a social media star

As the family has described in the bio section of their YouTube channel, their main goal is for parents to watch their videos and learn how to have fun with their children and other halves.

Their channel, “The Bee Family” was launched on 21 June 2013, and has since gathered well over nine million subscribers, and over 2.5 billion views of all their videos combined. Two of the family’s most popular videos are “GUMMY FOOD vs REAL FOOD!!” which has been viewed over 100 million times since 15 April 2017 and the “FORTNITE DANCE CHALLENGE! – (In Real Life)” which has been watched over 96 million times since 26 April 2018.

The family is also very active on Facebook, as they are followed by over 1.8 million people, while they’ve uploaded close to 2,000 pictures which have mostly been taken during their everyday lives.

They are least active on Twitter, but are followed by over 110,000 people, while they have more than 10 million fans on Facebook. Gabriela and the rest of the crew are taking extra care of their TikTok, onto which they upload at least one comedy video each day, and have thus gathered over 5.7 million followers and more than 82 million likes of all their videos and pictures combined.

Gabriela also has her own channel which she launched on 19 May 2018, and which is today subscribed to by over 1.6 million people, and counts close to 125 million views of all her videos combined – she is mostly focused on uploading her songs, gameplay videos and other random things.

On Instagram, Gabriela is followed by close to 700,000 people, and has 8,000 followers on Twitter. She is a TikTok star too, as she is followed by over six million fans, and has gathered over 126 million likes of all her videos and pictures combined.

Gabriela is also a singer, having released her single “By Myself” in 2019, while its music video was uploaded onto YouTube on 3 November 2019 – a few of the popular songs she has covered are “The Final Countdown”, “Dancing By Myself” and “High Hopes”.

Love life, relationships and friends

It is believed by many that Gabriela is still too young to be dating, but she is very secretive anyway when it comes to her love life, and hasn’t talked about any boys she might have dated.

However, there were two people who claimed to have seen Gabriela spending time with her high school friend, and that the two were holding hands – no pictures were provided to support their claims, and the post about this has already been deleted from the website Reddit.

Gabriela is a highly social person who has many friends, but spends most of her free time with her parents, as they all have to work together and create content for their numerous social media accounts. She has one best friend who can be seen featured in some of her pictures on Instagram, and who can on rare occasions also be seen featured in her family’s videos.

As of October 2020, Gabriela is single, hasn’t married and doesn’t have children.

Hobbies and other interests

Gabriela’s favorite season is winter as she prefers snow over sea, and is an avid skier while she also knows how to snowboard – the family spends at least one week each year high up in the mountains at their favorite ski resort.

She is keen on tasting various cuisines – her favorite is Italian while her favorite food is pizza.

Gabriela likes to travel and the family has been to several Canadian cities while they have also been to New York and Florida in the USA and France in Europe.

In her spare time and occasionally with her family, Gabriela likes to watch movies, and a few of her favorite actors and actresses are Johnny Depp, Will Smith and Natalie Portman, while a few of her favorite movies are the franchise “Pirates of the Caribbean”, “Bad Boys” and “Leon: The Professional”.

Age and height

Gabriela’s age is 14. She has long brown hair and brown eyes, her height is 5ft 3ins (1.6m) and she weighs around 120lbs (54kgs).

As of October 2020, her net worth has been estimated at over $1 million, while The Bee Family’s net worth has been estimated at over $6 million.
