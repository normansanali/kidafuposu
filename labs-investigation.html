Labs Investigation | DMZ Missions Guide in Warzone

Labs Investigation is a new Season 4 DMZ Mission for the faction Black Mous featured in the DMZ mode of Call of Duty: Warzone 2.

In this guide, you can find all the mission's objectives and the rewards you'll obtain by completing it.

Labs Investigation Mission Info

  • Rewards 556 Icarus (Contraband)
    +15.000 XP

Labs Investigation Mission Tasks:

  • Infill into Building 21
  • Investigate the Generator Room on F1
