Bray Wyatt Shows Off Big Change In His Look Before WWE Return

Bray Wyatt could be gearing up for a return, but he won’t look the same as he did the last time we saw the Eater Of Worlds. A picture of him in a barber chair shows him with a much different look.

Wyatt was happy to be sitting in Chriz Cutz’ chair as he got cleaned up. His head is half-shaved, but his dreadlocks are still there. He just has much less hair.

Perhaps this is a sign that Bray Wyatt is gearing up for a WWE return. He is rumored to be coming back soon and this might be a big sign that is coming very soon.

With WrestleMania coming up you never know if the Eater Of Worlds will be making his big return at the show of shows. Either way, he looks to be doing well as fans wait to see him make a return to the ring.
