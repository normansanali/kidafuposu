Yanet García Biography

Yanet García is a Mexican model, social media influence, actress, businesswoman, and TV presenter. García started her modeling career professionally when she was 15 years old.

Her strong drive and hard work helped her to open her own modeling business at the age of 20. Her modeling business goes by her name ‘Yanet García Models’. She explained that her mission with the organization is to assist young girls and teenagers in enhancing their self-esteem and achieving their goals.

Yanet became popular as a weather reporter on Las Noticias for Televisa Monterrey. She then rose to fame after the famous magazine ‘Playboy’ nominated her as the ‘Hottest Weather Girl’. Being a weather reporter at the news channel became the talk of the town due to her beautiful appearance. Often, she is compared to Kim Kardashian and Jennifer Lopez.

Later, she branched into acting where she was seen in the movie ‘Sharknado 5: Global Swarming’ in 2017. Her good looks combined with her industrious spirit gave rise to her status as a social media influencer. Currently, she has more than 500k subscribers.

How old is Yanet García? Yanet is 31 years old as of 2021. She was born on November 14, 1990, in Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, Mexico. Therefore, Yanet celebrates her birthday on November 14th every year and her zodiac sign is Scorpio.
