Al Bemiller Obituary, Funeral

The information reaching our outfit is that Albert Delane Bemiller popularly known as Al Bemiller has joined his ancestors.

Al Bemiller died on 30th November 2022 after battling Alzheimer’s disease for some years now. He died peacefully surrounded by his family. Al Bemiller, born on 18th April 1938, was an American Professional Footballer Player. He was an offensive lineman for the Buffalo Bills of the American Football League (AFL).

Al Bemiller died at the age of 84. He was an alumnus of Syracuse University and was inducted into the hall of fame in 2015. He was the husband of the late Wanda M. Bemillar and a brother to Dr. Carl Bemiller.

He is survived by three children, Todd Bemiller, Tambra Bemiller, Tanya A. Bemiller, and Tia Dolegala. His grandchildren are Lee Ashley, Lindsey Rae Bemiller, Jarrett Tyler (Christine), Jacob Taylor, and Jadd Tanner Dolegala.

According to the funeral arrangements, visitation will be held on Monday, December 5th, 2022 from 2:00 PM to 7:00 PM at the Hamburg Funeral Home (5453 Southwestern Blvd, Hamburg, NY 14075). His funeral will be held at the same venue for visitation on Tuesday, December 6th, 2022 at 10:00 AM.
